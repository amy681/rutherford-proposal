ROI Measurement Framework

GirlFriday tracks marketing performance using a defined measurement framework built around the KPIs established at contract launch. Social media metrics are monitored through native platform analytics and consolidated into a monthly dashboard. Website performance is tracked through Google Analytics, with traffic, session data, and conversion behavior reported monthly. Email marketing metrics including open rates, click-through rates, and list growth are tracked through the email platform and included in every monthly report. Event registration trends and membership movement are pulled directly from Wild Apricot and cross-referenced against prior periods. ROI is calculated by comparing campaign investment against measurable outcomes including event attendance growth, membership acquisition, and engagement benchmarks established at the start of the engagement. All reporting is delivered in a board-ready format every month without exception.
